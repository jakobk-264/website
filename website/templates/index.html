So what's this website about?

This is a place to share book reviews, article summaries, my personal take on a few ideas and some tutorials to improve productivity. The content is very much in the data science, credit risk, and model risk management space - and if possible at the intersection of all three.

Currently, I'm a risk analytics consultant and before that I was a model risk quant for credit risk and AI/ML models. Many of the content I'll cover is relevant to my day to day work, but I also go back and review fundamentals.
